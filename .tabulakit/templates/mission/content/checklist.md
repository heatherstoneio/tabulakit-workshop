# Planning Checklist

## Mission Analysis
- [ ] What is the mission/task?
- [ ] What is the desired end state?
- [ ] What are the constraints and limitations?
- [ ] What is the timeline?
- [ ] What resources are available?

## Personnel
- [ ] Who is required?
- [ ] Are they available for the required dates?
- [ ] Who are the key decision makers?
- [ ] What training or qualifications are needed?

## Logistics
- [ ] What equipment is needed?
- [ ] What facilities are required?
- [ ] What is the budget?
- [ ] What transportation is needed?
- [ ] What supplies need to be procured?

## Communications
- [ ] What is the primary communication method?
- [ ] What is the backup communication method?
- [ ] Who needs to be informed?
- [ ] What is the reporting schedule?

## Risk
- [ ] What are the key risks?
- [ ] What are the mitigation strategies?
- [ ] What is the contingency plan?
- [ ] Who is the safety officer?

## Coordination
- [ ] What external agencies/organizations are involved?
- [ ] What agreements or permissions are needed?
- [ ] What is the coordination timeline?
