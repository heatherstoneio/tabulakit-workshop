# Quick Reference

**Core principle:** You're overwhelmed because you're trying to hold everything in your head. Divide the work across domains (horizontal) and altitudes (vertical).

---

## The Seven Domains (S1-S7)

| Section | Domain | Key Question |
|---------|--------|--------------|
| **S1** | Personnel | Who do I have? What's their real status? |
| **S2** | Intelligence | What do I know vs. assume? |
| **S3** | Operations | What's the plan? What's the main effort? |
| **S4** | Logistics | What resources do I have? What can I sustain? |
| **S5** | Plans | What's the endgame? What does success look like? |
| **S6** | Signal | How does information flow? |
| **S7** | Info Ops | What narratives are in play — externally and in my own head? |

At small scale, one person may cover multiple domains. The sections serve as a **checklist** ensuring nothing is forgotten, not formal role assignments.

---

## The Three Altitudes

| Level | Focus | Timeframe | Common Trap |
|-------|-------|-----------|-------------|
| **Strategic** | Why | Years | Avoiding it because it's ambiguous |
| **Operational** | How | Months | Drowning here, losing sight of why |
| **Tactical** | What | Days | Defaulting here because it feels productive |

**Before engaging any decision, ask:** *"What altitude is this?"*

---

## The Orders Sequence

| Order | Plain Language | When | Message |
|-------|---------------|------|---------|
| **WARNO** | Advance notice | Before plan complete | "Here's what's coming" |
| **OPORD** | Full plan | Plan complete | "This is the plan" — includes purpose & success criteria |
| **FRAGO** | Change notice | Conditions change | "Here's what's different" |

**Discipline:** Warn → Brief → Update. Never skip steps. Always state intent.

---

## Execution Lifecycle

```
OPORD → Execution Brief → Execute → FRAGO (if needed) → AAR → Closure
```

| Phase | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **OPORD** | Define what to accomplish and why |
| **Execution Brief** | Confirm understanding (back-brief) |
| **Execute** | Do the work, track progress |
| **FRAGO** | Adapt when conditions change |
| **AAR** | What happened, why, what to improve |
| **Closure** | Document lessons, close out |

---

## Operating Rhythm

| Cadence | Purpose | Frequency |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| **Daily standup** | What's happening today, blockers | Daily |
| **Domain sync** | Cross-functional coordination | Weekly |
| **Commander's update** | Strategic alignment, priority decisions | Weekly |
| **Planning cycle** | Look ahead, adjust course | Monthly |
| **AAR** | Learn from execution | After each operation |
