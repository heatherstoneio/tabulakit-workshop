# Participant Guide

## What to Expect

This is a hands-on workshop. You won't be sitting and watching slides — you'll be building something real for your business, with live support every step of the way.

**The morning intro** (free, 1 hour) is a presentation. Just show up and listen. No laptop needed.

**The afternoon workshop** (paid, 4 hours) is where you build. Bring your laptop and be ready to work. You'll type, click, and talk to AI — and by the end, you'll have a working system.

## How It Works

You'll be working with an AI assistant called Claude Code. Instead of chatting in a text box, Claude Code works inside your actual project — it can read your files, create new ones, run commands, and deploy your site. You tell it what you want in plain English, and it does the technical work.

Think of it like having a very capable intern sitting next to you who happens to know everything about building websites and organizing information.

## What If I'm Not Technical?

That's exactly who this workshop is for. You don't need to know how to code. You don't need to understand databases or servers. You need to be able to:

- Type on a keyboard
- Follow step-by-step instructions
- Describe what you want in plain English

Everything else, we'll handle together.

## What If I Get Stuck?

Raise your hand. Seriously. Getting stuck is expected and normal. The instructor will be circulating the room specifically to help people who hit walls. There's no penalty for asking questions — that's the whole point of a live workshop.

## What to Bring

- **Your laptop** (charged, with charger just in case)
- **Pre-work completed** — [see the checklist](pre-work.md)
- **An idea** of what you want to organize or build
- **Any existing materials** — documents, spreadsheets, notes that relate to what you want to build
- **Patience and curiosity** — some steps will feel unfamiliar, and that's okay

## What You'll Leave With

By the end of the day, you'll have:
- A working AI environment on your laptop
- A live website with your business content
- Skills and cheat sheets to keep building on your own
- A community of fellow workshop alumni

This isn't a one-day thing that fades. The tools and skills you learn are yours to keep and build on.
