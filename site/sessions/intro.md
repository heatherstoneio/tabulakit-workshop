# Free Intro: AI for Your Business

*1 hour — presentation + Q&A | 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM*

No laptop required. No commitment. Just a clear-eyed look at what AI can actually do for your business.

## What We'll Cover

### How You're Already Using AI (10 min)

Most people use AI like a fancy Google search — type a question, get an answer, copy-paste it somewhere. That works, but you're leaving 90% of the value on the table. We'll look at what you're probably doing today and what you're missing.

### What's Actually Happening Inside (10 min)

You don't need to be an engineer to understand how AI works. In plain English: it predicts the next word. That one idea — really understood — changes how you use it. We'll cover the core mechanism and why it produces the behavior you see every day.

### Where AI Falls Down (10 min)

AI confidently gives wrong answers. It forgets what you told it five minutes ago. It doesn't know what happened last week. These aren't bugs — they're predictable from how it works. Once you understand why, you can work around them instead of being surprised by them.

### A Better Way to Work With AI (15 min)

There's a gap between "chatting with AI" and "building with AI." We'll look at the document-first approach: instead of starting every conversation from scratch, you give AI a persistent project context. It gets smarter as your project grows. Live demo of a real business workflow.

### Q&A (10 min)

Open floor. Ask anything.

## Key Takeaways

1. **AI is powerful but most people are underusing it** — like using a race car to go to the grocery store
2. **Understanding the mechanism makes you dramatically better** — even at a high level
3. **The gap between chatting and building is the workshop** — that's where the real value is
4. **You don't need to be technical — you need to be structured**
